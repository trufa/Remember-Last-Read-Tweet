Remember Last Read Tweet

Mark your last read tweet and go right back to it when you get back.

How to use

Simply click on the "last" link to save the tweet as last and the click on "Go to last" whenever you return, the extension will scroll you to the saved tweet.

Install it from the Chrome Web Store here!
